Why learn Computer Science?

Computer Science is a growing technological field that offers students the opportunity to study the design, development, and analysis of software and hardware to solve real-world problems. It has various applications in all fields from data science to medical services, so acquiring these technical skills will offer

High Salaries

According to the US Bureau of Labor Statistics, the number of computer science jobs are projected to increase by 15%, much higher than average. So, there will be many job opportunties for students who pursue this career.

Increasing Job Opportunities

According to Glassdoor, the average computer scientist makes about $104,615 annually which is much higher than the average income. So, pursuing computer science will ensure financial security for the future.

Career Satisfaction

In the Stack Overflow Developer Survey 2019 about 74.3% of programmers reported being satisfied with their job. So, students should not hesitate to pursue computer science since most employees report enjoying their work and work environment.
